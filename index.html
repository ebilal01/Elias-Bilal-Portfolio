Welcome

My name is Elias, a first generation student at Tufts University in the class of 2026 pursuing a degree in Mechical Engineering. I also serve as President of National Society of Black Engineers, compete as an athlete on Tufts Football Team, and work as a Research Assistant in the Microscale Sensors and Systems Lab. I coded this portfolio to keep an organized display of my projects and artworks while helping others get to know more about my skills and interests.
